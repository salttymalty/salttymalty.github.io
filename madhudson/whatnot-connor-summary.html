Everything we've built on the SEO side is catching infrastructure. Whatnot streams are the demand source that fills the net.

→ Whatnot streams generate discovery (65–100 viewers per session)

The SEO work (product schema, GSC monitoring, nav structure, Merchant Center) built the net. Without it, stream-generated demand leaks. Without streams, the net catches ~200 sessions/day of ambient traffic. Together: 800–1,200 on stream days.

What the Research Found

We researched Whatnot's programs, fees, market size, and comparable brands. Three things matter most.

The Brad video is a conversation starter, not a trade chip

Whatnot has no formal "trade content for ad placement" program. Nobody's done it publicly. The video is still valuable — frame it as "here's our story" when you talk to your contact. Be open to Ambassador program, affiliate boost, or featured placement — just don't expect a direct content-for-credits exchange.

Fee negotiation unlocks at $10K/month

Standard fees: 8% commission + 2.9% + $0.30 per sale (~$13.60 per $95 watch). Not negotiable at current volume (~$3.2K/month). Sellers at $10K+ report success getting promotional credits or reduced rates from the seller success team. That's 3–5 streams/month consistently.

You're probably one of the only artist-collab watch brands on the platform

Watches aren't even a dedicated Whatnot category (filed under Collectibles). The platform is dominated by resellers, not original brands. Artist-collaboration watches would be rare and notable. Worth a quick browse to confirm.

The Closest Parallel: Naadam on QVC

Cashmere brand Naadam runs the same two-channel model. They sell $100–250K in 5–10 minute QVC spots at wholesale-adjacent pricing, then their website handles full retail + brand story. They even create QVC-exclusive colorways to prevent channel cannibalization.

The lesson: the website is not optional, it's the moat. Every Whatnot discovery should funnel to a story, not just a buy button.

Consider: Whatnot-exclusive colorways could prevent price comparison between channels.
