RF & Wireless

Lessons from Previous Gigs

Antenna separation: Keep ULXD4Q receivers away from any venue PA transmitters or wireless systems. At Holy Family, intermod + audience RF absorption caused dropouts.

Pre-prep: At Egan-Michelle you lost 35 min to on-site RF coordination. Program packs before arriving. On-site = verify scan + adjust only.

Use the downtime between ceremony setup and ceremony start (~2:00–3:30) to scan, assign frequencies, and test.
