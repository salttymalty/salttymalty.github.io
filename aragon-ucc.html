RF & Wireless
Lessons from Previous Gigs

Antenna separation: Keep ULXD4Q receivers away from any venue PA transmitters or wireless systems. At Holy Family, intermod + audience RF absorption caused dropouts.

Pre-prep: At Egan-Michelle you lost 35 min to on-site RF coordination. On-site = verify scan + adjust only.

Use 2:00–4:00 PM to scan, assign frequencies, and walk-test. Mount helical antenna HIGH with line-of-sight to stage, AWAY from LA4X amp.
