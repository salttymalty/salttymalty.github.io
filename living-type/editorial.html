The Variable Issue

A variable font carries not one design but an entire design space — a continuous field of possibility compressed into a single file. Weight, width, optical size: these are not static values but axes of expression, tunable to context.

The optical size axis is the quietest revolution. At reading scale, strokes thicken and spacing opens; at display scale, contrast sharpens and details assert themselves. The same typeface, reading its own environment.

What changes when type breathes? The eye does not catch the motion — it only registers ease. Good variable typography disappears into the reading experience, making everything feel more exactly right.
